The match rule

We don't apply to everything.
Here's how we choose.

Every role is scored against your target before we touch it. Volume for its own sake wastes your name. This is the filter.

Below 70% match

We skip it.

If a role isn't a real fit, your name doesn't go on it. We don't pad your total with junk to hit a number.

70–80% match

Master resume.

Close-fit roles get your master resume, built to carry you across a range of strong matches, applied fast.

Above 80% match

Full rewrite.

Strong matches get a complete resume rewrite against that exact job description. This is where interviews come from.

That's why our application numbers look lower than the bots'. Every application here is a real decision, not a total we're inflating.

FAQ

Questions worth asking
before you sign up.

Do you guarantee a job?

No, and anyone who does is lying to you. We guarantee structure: a sharper profile, clearer positioning, better recruiter visibility, and a job search process you can actually manage. Final hiring depends on company requirements, competition, salary expectations and interview performance.

Which countries do you support?

Wherever you're applying to English-language roles, we can run your search. We format resumes for every major market: India, the Gulf, UK, Europe, North America and Australia, and adapt positioning to each. One honest note: language-specific applications, like a German-language Lebenslauf and cover letter, aren't offered yet. If that affects your target roles, we'll tell you upfront.

Do you really work with clients anywhere?

Yes. The system is location-independent. What matters is that your target roles are in English and in a market we can format for, which now covers most of the world. If we're not the right fit for your specific market, we'll say so before you pay.

Do you apply manually?

Yes, every application is a real human decision. No auto-apply bots, no spam, no spray-and-pray. We apply to roles that actually match your level, geography and direction.

Why are your application numbers lower than auto-apply tools?

Because we don't apply to everything. Roles below a 70% match don't get your name. Between 70 and 80% we use your master resume; above 80% we rewrite it fully for that job. Fewer applications, far higher fit. A bot firing 500 generic applications isn't competition, it's noise.

Can freshers use this service?

Yes. Freshers usually start with the one-time Resume (all levels) or LinkedIn Profile Plus, and move to Starter when they want the full application engine.

What do I need to send for a free review?

Your current resume, LinkedIn URL, target job titles, target country, notice period, and your main job search concern. That's enough for us to spot what's working and what isn't.

How long until I see results?

Profile work (resume + LinkedIn) is usually visible within the first week. Application response patterns become clear by week 2–3. We track and adjust weekly.

What if I'm on a notice period?

This is exactly what Growth and Global Premium are built for. You focus on your current job. We manage the structured search in the background: applications, outreach, tracking, weekly review.
